Project Overview

Modern Global Payment Platform

ClearerPay is a comprehensive fintech platform enabling seamless cross-border payments and currency exchange. The platform simplifies collections and makes payments globally with ease through multi-currency accounts.

As the new standard for payments across the world, ClearerPay's gateway simplifies collections with low fees and no hidden charges, helping businesses save costs and accept payments effortlessly across 40+ currencies.

Platform Capabilities

Multi-Currency Digital Wallet

40+ Currencies Real-time Rates Live Balance

Carry out transactions in USD, EUR, GBP and 40+ other currencies with real-time exchange rates. Our multi-currency account equips your business to pay, get paid, and grow globally with competitive rates.

Named Payout & Virtual Accounts

Business Name Settlement Virtual Accounts Direct Integration

Settle payments in your business name with direct integration, eliminating middlemen and third-party risks. Get unique virtual accounts created in your business's name to receive payments from clients worldwide.

Card Processing & Global Collections

Debit/Credit Cards Bank Transfers Digital Wallets

Accept payments from credit or debit cards online with automatic currency conversion. Receive payments from customers globally through various methods like bank transfers, digital wallets, and cards with foreign exchange capabilities.

High-Performance Transaction Dashboard

Real-time Data OTC Transactions Advanced Analytics

Built high-performance dashboards capable of rendering real-time data for over ₦20M+ in daily OTC transactions. Implemented advanced error handling, optimistic UI updates, and real-time balance tracking.
